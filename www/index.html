    This is your miners inventory. Any elements you find manually will be stored here.

    Your inventory is broken out in to pages. Click the arrows to view other item types.

    This panel displays information about your fleet, drones, and more.

  3. Stats

    This panel displays stats on how many times you have mined, gathered, and scavenged.

  6. Mining

    Click this button to manually mine.
    You also can press/hold D.
    There is a chance to find elements when mining
    Craft a better pickaxe for a higher chance of finding elements, and finding more at once.

  7. Gathering

    Click this button to manually gather atmosphere.

    Many gases are used in crafting, and oxygen is required for space travel.

  8. Scavenging

    Click this button to scavenge for materials.

    Scavenged items can be decomposed to provide base materials for use in crafting

  9. Gear

    This panel displays the gear you have equipped, as well as your characters stats.

    Right click to equip, or unequip gear.

  10. Ship

    In Development

    This panel displays information related to your ship, such as fuel levels.

  11. Planet

    This panel displays what elements your buildings have found on the planet.

    Any elements found when using a building will go in to your planet storage.

    Items in your planet storage must be transferred manually.

  12. Crafting

    This panel displays the recipes used in crafting.
